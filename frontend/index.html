        About

        Built for Farmers,
        Powered by AI

        CropSense AI is an intelligent crop disease monitoring and decision support system designed to help farmers identify diseases early, track progression over time, understand risks, and take prompt, data-driven action to protect their harvest.

        Using a multi-task MobileNetV2 architecture with multi-modal fusion (image + weather + soil), the model provides accurate predictions with explainable AI transparency. The AI assistant analyzes all available data to generate personalized recommendations.
